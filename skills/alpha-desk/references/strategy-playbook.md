## Core Approach: Quality Momentum

Buy sector leaders riding secular trends. Enter on pullbacks or confirmed breakouts. Hold until thesis breaks, target hit, or better opportunity arises.

## Entry Criteria (must meet 3+ of these)

- [ ] ROCE > 15% (capital efficiency)
- [ ] Revenue growth > 10% YoY (growth)
- [ ] Not at all-time high OR volume-confirmed breakout (timing)
- [ ] Clear catalyst in next 1-3 months (urgency)
- [ ] Sector in uptrend or mean-reverting from oversold (environment)
- [ ] Risk-reward > 3:1 from current price to target vs stop (math)

## Exit Rules

| Trigger | Action | Exception |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| Target hit | Sell 50%, trail rest | If momentum is extreme, trail all |
| Stop loss hit | Sell 100% immediately | None. No exceptions. Ever. |
| Thesis broken | Sell regardless of P&L | Even if in profit — dead thesis = dead trade |
| Better opportunity | Sell weakest holding | Only if new idea is clearly superior |
| +10% gain | Move stop to breakeven | Locks in "free trade" |
| +20% gain | Move stop to +10% | Protect profits |

## Position Management

- New position: 5-10% of portfolio
- High conviction: Up to 15%
- Average down: Only if thesis is STRONGER (not just because it's cheaper)
- Average up: Yes, if breakout is confirmed on volume
- Diversification: Minimum 5 holdings, maximum 12

## Market Regime Rules

| Regime | Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Risk-on | VIX < 15, breadth expanding, Fed dovish | Full allocation, growth bias |
| Neutral | Mixed signals | Standard allocation, balanced |
| Risk-off | VIX > 25, breadth narrowing, Fed hawkish | Raise cash to 30%+, defensive bias |
| Crisis | VIX > 35, correlations spike | 50%+ cash, only buy extreme oversold quality |
